MOBA Games

Dota 2

Dota 2 is a multiplayer online battle arena game from Valve Corporation, where two teams of five players compete to destroy the opposing team's ancient, located within their base, while defending their own.

League of Legends

League of Legends is a fast-paced MOBA where two teams of five champions, each with unique abilities, battle to destroy the other team's Nexus, situated at the heart of their base, while defending their own.

Mobile Legends

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang is a popular mobile MOBA game where two teams of five players engage in strategic battles to destroy the enemy's base and achieve victory, featuring fast matchmaking and intense gameplay.
