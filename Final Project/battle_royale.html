Battle Royale Games

PUBG

PUBG, or PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds, is a battle royale game where 100 players parachute onto an island and scavenge for weapons and equipment to eliminate others while avoiding getting killed themselves.

Warzone

Call of Duty: Warzone is a free-to-play battle royale game that combines the elements of the Call of Duty series with large-scale, last-man-standing gameplay, featuring both Battle Royale and Plunder modes.

Fortnite

Fortnite is a popular battle royale game that features a unique building mechanic, allowing players to construct defensive structures while battling to be the last person or team standing on a constantly shrinking map.
